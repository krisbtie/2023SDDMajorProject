Education facts in 2021 census

The 2021 Census showed that Australians are well-educated, with 72% of people having completed Year 12 or equivalent. This is higher than the OECD average of 64%. This shows that Australia is a highly educated country, and the level of educational attainment is increasing over time. This is good news for Australia, as it means that we have a well-educated workforce that is well-equipped to compete in the global economy.

Instituion by Male, Female as at 2021 Census

As per the 2021 census, more females than males were attending an educational institution. Males were more likely to be attending a vocational education institution than females , while females were more likely to be attending a university or other higher education institution than males.
